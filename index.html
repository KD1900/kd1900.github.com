About Me

I am a 3th-year M.S. candidate at the College of Computer Science and Technology, Huaqiao University and my adviser is Yiqiao Cai. Previously, I received my B.S. degree from Quanzhou Normal University in 2012. The best way to reach me is through email.

I have broad interests in artificial intelligence, including machine learning and evolutionary computation. Currently, I am focusing on how to improve the performance of differential evolution with the population information, expecially, how to improve the mutation operators with static neighborhood learning strategy and dynamic neighborhood learning strategy. Our research work has been published in international journals and conferences including Soft Computing, 3PGCIC, etc..
